FAQs

How do I reset my password?

You can reset your password by clicking on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and following the instructions sent to your email.

Can I export student reports?

Yes, you can export student reports in multiple formats including PDF, CSV, and Excel directly from the reports section.

Is there a mobile app available?

Yes, our mobile app is available for both iOS and Android devices. You can download it from the App Store or Google Play Store.
